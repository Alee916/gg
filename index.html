Two Sides of Energy. One Integrated Strategy.

Supply + Efficiency = Maximum Savings

Most companies attack energy costs from one angle—either they negotiate better rates or they reduce consumption. We do both, and that's why our clients see savings that single-solution providers can't deliver.

As a wholesale energy aggregator, we secure supply rates individual businesses can't negotiate alone. As a licensed energy efficiency general contractor, we reduce what you actually consume. These strategies work together, compounding your savings in ways that neither approach can achieve on its own.

Wholesale Aggregation

We pool buying power across hundreds of accounts to access wholesale energy markets. Independent supplier evaluation, strategic hedging, and real-time market monitoring—all working to lower your supply costs.

Efficiency Contracting

As a licensed energy efficiency GC, we handle everything from audits to installation. LED lighting, HVAC upgrades, building automation, weatherization—we manage the project and maximize your utility incentive capture.

Incentive Capture

Utility rebates and incentives can cover 30-70% of upgrade costs—but only if you know how to capture them. We navigate the programs, handle the paperwork, and ensure you get every dollar available for your project.
